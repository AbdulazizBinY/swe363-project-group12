Welcome to KFUPM CourseCraft

Your ultimate tool for academic planning at KFUPM.

What is KFUPM CourseCraft?

KFUPM CourseCraft is a web application designed to enhance the academic planning and resource management experience for students at King Fahd University of Petroleum and Minerals (KFUPM). This application addresses several issues with the current system, known as the Student Educational Planner (SEP), which students find not user-friendly and difficult to use.

Why KFUPM CourseCraft?

  1. Improved User Experience: Offering a smoother user experience for students while planning their future academic terms.
  2. Centralized Resource Access: Combining useful resources in one place, making it easier for students to find and utilize them.
  3. Key Requirements:
    • Academic Planning: Enable students to develop and modify their academic plans, search for courses, and check for scheduling conflicts and prerequisite fulfillment.
    • Course Resource Access: Centralize access to course materials and ensure ease of finding necessary study materials.
    • Instructor Evaluations: Allow students to provide feedback on instructors, and view evaluations made by others.
    • Administrative Features: Provide administrators with tools to moderate content, handle technical issues, and manage the platform.
  4. Non-functional Requirements: The platform aims to be user-friendly, responsive, secure, capable of handling a growing number of users, and accessible from various devices.

Schedule

In the context of academic planning, a schedule would typically a way to plan and organize of courses over a semester or academic year. This could involve checking for scheduling conflicts.

Resources

Resources in KFUPM CourseCraft primarily include course-related materials. These could be lecture notes, study guides, past exam papers, or any other educational content relevant to the courses offered at KFUPM.

  • Functionality for Students: Students can download available resources for their courses.
  • Process: The student, upon logging in and navigating to the resources section, selects the resource they wish to download. The system then initiates the download, and the student receives the downloaded resource.
